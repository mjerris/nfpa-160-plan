# Entity 11: Inspection Schedule

Based on Annex C (informational but strongly recommended).

## 11.1 Temporary Installations

### Daily Inspection [C.2.1.1]

| Item | Section |
|---|---|
| Visual inspection of fuel storage area (secured, housekeeping, no unrelated items, access) | [C.2.1.1(1)] |
| Visual inspection of performance area(s) (housekeeping, flammable materials, props, access) | [C.2.1.1(2)] |
| Visual inspection of fire effects equipment (components functional, damage noted/repaired) | [C.2.1.1(3)] |
| Daily preset: operate effects per designer procedure to verify enable and arming | [C.2.1.1(4)] |
| Verify emergency shutdown provisions, fuel management, fire suppression present/operating | [C.2.1.1(5)] |
| Observe effect operation during show to verify expected operating profile | [C.2.1.1(6)] |

### Weekly and Monthly Inspection [C.2.1.2]

| Item | Section |
|---|---|
| As dictated by design, operation, and duration of temporary installation | [C.2.1.2] |

## 11.2 Permanent Installations

### Daily Inspection [C.2.2.1]

| Item | Section |
|---|---|
| Visual inspection of fuel storage area (secured, housekeeping, no unrelated items, access) | [C.2.2.1(1)] |
| Visual inspection of performance area(s) (housekeeping, flammable materials, props, access) | [C.2.2.1(2)] |
| Visual inspection of fire effects equipment (functional, damage noted/repaired) | [C.2.2.1(3)] |
| Verify primary limits in proper control profile and indicating proper operating level (LEL sensors, airflow switches, fuel pressure switches, etc.) | [C.2.2.1(4)] |
| Daily preset: operate effects per designer procedure to verify enable and arming | [C.2.2.1(5)] |
| Verify emergency shutdown provisions, fuel management, fire suppression present/operating | [C.2.2.1(6)] |
| Observe effect operation during show to verify expected operating profile | [C.2.2.1(7)] |

### Monthly Inspection [C.2.2.2]

| Item | Section |
|---|---|
| Test and calibrate limit/interlock control devices subject to drift over time (per manufacturer) | [C.2.2.2(1)] |
| Leak test of primary fuel supply shutoff valves (per manufacturer) | [C.2.2.2(2)] |
| Verify intrusion and/or position interlocks operating properly with correct control system response | [C.2.2.2(3)] |
| Other test sequences per effects designer or cognizant individual | [C.2.2.2(4)] |

### Quarterly Inspection [C.2.2.3]

| Item | Section |
|---|---|
| Calibrate all primary limit/interlock devices subject to drift | [C.2.2.3(1)] |
| Actuate all primary limit/interlock devices to confirm function, response, and actuation confirmation | [C.2.2.3(2)] |
| Actuate central fire alarm to verify emergency stop of effects | [C.2.2.3(3)] |
| Actuate all emergency stop operators to confirm function; selected stops actuated during operation to verify proper shutdown | [C.2.2.3(4)] |
| Operate fuel supply enable circuit to verify fuel limits and valves | [C.2.2.3(5)] |
| Enable and arm random effects (min 3 or 20% of total) to verify ignition management: safe start check, trial for ignition, flame failure response time, non-recycling. If multiple zones, test â‰¥1 per zone | [C.2.2.3(6)] |
| Test PME during operation: effects must not enable/arm/fire without PME; removal during sequence must shut down | [C.2.2.3(7)] |
| Operate effects in normal show mode to verify enable/arm/fire sequence per normal profile | [C.2.2.3(8)] |
| Other test sequences per effects designer or cognizant individual | [C.2.2.3(9)] |

### Annual Inspection [C.2.2.4]

| Item | Section |
|---|---|
| Complete verification of all effects control components | [C.2.2.4] |
| Serves as certification for operating permit renewal | [C.2.2.4] |
| Includes all daily, monthly, and quarterly sequences to extent that ALL components are tested | [C.2.2.4] |
| Additional tests per effects designer or cognizant individual | [C.2.2.4] |
